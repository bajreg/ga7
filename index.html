Reproducible Metric Calc (Code)

# Rolling Net Interest Margin (NIM)
import pandas as pd

# df columns: ['date','interest_income','interest_expense','earning_assets']
df['nim'] = (df['interest_income'] - df['interest_expense']) / df['earning_assets']
# 4-quarter rolling average
nim_rolling = df.set_index('date')['nim'].rolling(window=4).mean()
print(nim_rolling.tail())

Finance Math

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} - C_0 \]

Cost of Equity (CAPM):

\[ k_e = r_f + \beta (r_m - r_f) \]

Effective Annual Rate (EAR):

\[ \mathrm{EAR} = \left(1 + \frac{i}{n}\right)^n - 1 \]

Define each symbol: r is discount rate, C_t cash flow at time t, C_0 initial investment. For CAPM, confirm beta window and market proxy.
